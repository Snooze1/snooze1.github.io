    Stepcharts

    Dance games have been in my life for as long as I can remember, however my more active involvement in the scene got started around 2013. The open source nature of the community allowed me to get involved not just as a player but as a creator. I've made dozens of arrow choreographies for songs (called stepcharts in the community) over the years. Some have even landed spots for use in international tournaments!
