    Stepcharts

    Dance games have been a part of my life as long as I can remember. Some of my earliest memories are hopping around on the DDR machine at the arcade, or slip sliding around on the paper home pads. My passion for the game was really ignited around 2013 however, bringing me all the way to the modern scene today.

    The open source nature of the community allowed me to get involved not just as a player but as a creator. I've made dozens of charts over the years, some even landing spots in international tournaments! I'm so happy to be able to share the joy of this game with so many.
