ITG Charts

Hi!

I've been creating charts for an community driven rhythm game called "In The Groove" (and in the more modern meta, ITL) since about 2016 or so. In the last year or so I've catalogued onto a spreadsheet the 100+ unique charts I've created with relevant metadata. I wanted to bring it all together into a primary hub where they can be archived and accessed. I hope this page can evolve to include more as time goes on, but for now, please enjoy the content I have to offer.
